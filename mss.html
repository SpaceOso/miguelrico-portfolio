Was tasked with converting our mySmartHealthcare.com courses to match the ones already on Healthstream. This required going through the javascript files that procedurally create our courses and understanding how it works to then style it and rearrange the DOM.

All scene models and lighting in this image besides patient, bed and equipment attached to patient were created by me in 3ds Max. Render and scene dress up by Ben Barnard

Using jQuery and jPlayer for the voice over, I created the tutorial interaction which goes over the GUI features.

There was various interactions I had to recreate, such as a drag and drop interaction. Click to show interactions and a quiz interaction.

Source for interactions provided by Healthstream.
